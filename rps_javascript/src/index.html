Rules

Hi. Please play my 'Rock. Papper Scissors' game. If you have never played before, the rules are as folows:

    • You can choose between Rock, Papper and Scissors.
    • Rock < Scissors
    • Rock < Papper
    • Scissors < Papper
    • A player gets a point if their choice is greater than their opponents.
      You can play as many times as you want, but normally, the first to get three points wins. For fareness,
      please look away from the screen while the other player makes their choice.
